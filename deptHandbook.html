Mission

This Department aims to provide:

  • Every Student
    • The knowledge and skills needed to be a safe user of technologies
    • The knowledge and skills to be a digitally connected citizen
    • The knowledge and skills in ICT needed to enjoy success in other academic areas.
    • The knowledge and skills to obtain their best achievement in ICT and Computing.
  • Every Teacher
    • With the skills needed to engage every student by incorporating an appropriate level of ICT.
